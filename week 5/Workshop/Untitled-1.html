Pashupatinath: Nepal's revered Hindu temple complex!

Pashupatinath is a highly sacred Hindu temple located in Kathmandu, Nepal. It is devoted to Pashupatinath, a manifestation of the god Shiva. The temple attracts numerous pilgrims annually and has been designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
